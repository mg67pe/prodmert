Absorption Kinetics

Sour Gas (Green): Enters from the bottom loaded with highly corrosive and toxic H₂S/CO₂.

Lean Amine (Light Purple): MDEA or DEA solution pumped from the top. It selectively binds with acid gases.

Sweet Gas (Blue): Clean methane exits the top, ready for the dehydration unit or pipeline.
