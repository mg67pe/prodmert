Drillpipe (DP)

Primary Function:

Transmits rotation (RPM) and weight (WOB) to the bit. Circulates drilling mud.

Attached Equipment (BHA):
  • Drill Bit: Cuts the rock.
  • Mud Motor: Steers and spins the bit.
  • MWD/LWD: Logging tools for survey data.
  • Drill Collars: Heavy pipes providing weight.
