Cushion Gas: the permanent base volume (rock/brine pressure support) that is never withdrawn — keeps deliverability and prevents water/salt influx into the wellbore.
Working Gas: the commercially injectable/withdrawable volume cycled seasonally to balance summer supply surplus against winter demand peaks.
