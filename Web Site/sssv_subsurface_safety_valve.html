Hydraulic Actuation

The SSSV is held open by hydraulic pressure pumped from the surface through a control line. If pressure is lost (e.g., umbilical severed or ESD triggered), the Power Spring forces the flow tube up, allowing the Flapper to snap shut and secure the well.
