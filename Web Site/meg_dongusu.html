MEG Closed Loop System (Flow Assurance)

The process of antifreeze traveling downhole and being regenerated to prevent hydrate (ice) formation

Lean MEG (Pure Antifreeze)

Pumped from the surface at high pressure via the Umbilical to the Subsea Tree/Choke.

Rich MEG (Watery Antifreeze)

Mixed with formation water, it returns to the surface along with the gas through the Flowline.
