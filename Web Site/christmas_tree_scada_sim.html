Equipment Encyclopedia

Master Valve (LMV/UMV)

The primary safety barrier. It isolates the entire wellbore from the surface. Must be FULLY OPEN during production. Never used to throttle flow, as erosion would destroy its sealing capability during an emergency.

Production Wing (PWV)

The daily operational valve. Routes the gas to the flowline and choke. Closed for routine maintenance or when shutting the well in temporarily.

Swab Valve

The topmost vertical valve. Allows straight-line access downhole. Opened ONLY when a Lubricator is attached to perform Wireline/Coiled Tubing interventions.

Kill Wing (KWV)

Connected to the rig's mud pumps. Opened only to pump heavy "Kill Fluid" down the well to suppress reservoir pressure (kill the well) during emergencies.
