Ice Plugging in Subsea Flowlines and P-T Phase Analysis

Operation Control

The seabed is very cold (~4°C). As gas flows through the pipeline, it can form Hydrates (solid ice-like crystals). By injecting MEG (Monoethylene Glycol), we suppress the hydrate formation temperature (shifts the curve left).

Flowline Pressure (P) 2000 psia
Flowline Temperature (T) 15 °C
MEG Injection Rate 0% (Pure Water)

Shifts the equilibrium curve to colder temperatures, protecting the system.
