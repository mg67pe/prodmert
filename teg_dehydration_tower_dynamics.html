Mass Transfer Theory

Counter-Current Flow: Gas rises through the bubble caps, creating a froth layer in the liquid TEG pooling on each tray.

Absorption: TEG is highly hygroscopic. As gas bubbles through it, water molecules (white) transfer from the gas phase (blue) into the liquid TEG phase (green turning yellow/brown).
